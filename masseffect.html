A little bit of information about the story of Mass Effect

Mass Effect takes place after Humanity discovers alien ruins on Mars which propels human technology to the point they are able to travel distant stars. There they come across and join a galactic community all the while sinister forces work in the background leading your character do discover a dark secret that dates back billions of years.
