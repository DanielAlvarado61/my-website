A little bit of information about the story of Fallout

Fallout is set in a grim post apocalyptic world. On October 23rd, 2077 the war between the United States of America and China came too an end when both countries launched their nuclear weapons. All the games take place in post war America where you face off against a varitey of mutated creatures, mutants, raiders, and hostile factions of post war survivors. There are seven fallout video games and a tv show on Prime Video. The history and lore of the fallout universe is very deep if you are interested in learning more there is a link at the bottom of the page where you can do so.
