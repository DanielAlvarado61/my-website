How I Started Playing Video Games

My first console was a Nintendo 64 which I shared with my older sister and our favorite game to play was 007 Golden Eye. My next console was playstation 2, then in 2007 the release of Halo 3 lead me to getting my first xbox 360 with live service. Video games hold fond memories for me playing them with friends and my family. More than that I enjoyed the visual storytelling elements mixed with the ability to actually participate in it. Besides the main video game consoles I also enjoyed playing Pokemon on my gameboy during long drives to my grandparents house.
